This guide walks you through browsing Pedal's vehicle catalog and submitting your first reservation using the booking form on the homepage.

<Info>
  No account is required to browse vehicles or fill in the booking form. You can start a reservation immediately from the homepage.
</Info>

## Make your first reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Pedal website and browse the catalog">
    Open the Pedal homepage. The top navigation bar contains five sections:

    - **Inicio** — Homepage with the booking form
    - **Cómo funciona** — How the rental process works
    - **Alquiler** — Vehicle catalog
    - **¿Por qué elegirnos?** — Service features
    - **Contáctanos** — Newsletter and contact

    Scroll down to the **Alquiler** section or click **Alquiler** in the nav to reach the vehicle catalog.

    <Tip>
      The catalog loads with Honda vehicles displayed by default. If you're browsing for the first time, this is a good starting point to get a sense of the price range and specs.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter vehicles by brand">
    At the top of the catalog, you'll see five brand filter buttons:

    - **Honda**
    - **Chevrolet**
    - **Volvo**
    - **Mitsubishi**
    - **Volkswagen**

    Click any brand button to display only that brand's vehicles. The catalog updates immediately to show the matching models.

    Each vehicle card displays:
    - Fuel type (Petrol or Diesel)
    - Number of seats
    - Transmission type (Automático or Manual)
    - Fuel efficiency (km per liter)
    - Daily rental price starting from a base rate in Soles (S/.)

    <Note>
      Prices shown are starting rates per day. The full price for your reservation depends on the number of days and any additional options at pickup.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Rentar ahora' on your chosen vehicle">
    Once you find a vehicle that suits your needs, click the **Rentar ahora** button on its card.

    This scrolls you back to the booking form at the top of the page, where you'll fill in your reservation details.

    <Tip>
      If you want more information before committing, click the **Detalles** button on the vehicle card first.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the booking form">
    The booking form has six fields organized in three rows. Complete all fields before submitting.

    **Row 1 — Car type and date**

    - **Selecciona tu tipo de carro** — Choose from:
      - Compacto
      - Sedán
      - SUV
      - Camioneta
    - **Fecha de uso** — Select the calendar date for your rental using the date picker.

    **Row 2 — Pickup details**

    - **Hora de recojo** — Set the time you will collect the vehicle.
    - **Lugar de recojo** — Choose your pickup location:
      - Miraflores
      - San Isidro
      - San Miguel

    **Row 3 — Return details**

    - **Hora de devolución** — Set the time you will return the vehicle.
    - **Lugar de devolución** — Choose your return location:
      - Miraflores
      - San Isidro
      - San Miguel

    <Info>
      Your pickup location and return location can be different. For example, you can pick up in Miraflores and return in San Miguel.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Once all six fields are filled in, click the **Enviar** button at the bottom of the form to submit your reservation request.

    <Note>
      After submitting, Pedal's team will process your request. If you have questions or need to make changes, contact support — available 24/7.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
