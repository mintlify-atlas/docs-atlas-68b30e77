## Welcome to Pedal

Pedal is a consumer-facing car rental platform designed for drivers in Peru. Whether you need a compact city car for a day in Lima or a spacious SUV for a weekend getaway, Pedal connects you with the right vehicle, at the right time, in the right location.

## Available vehicle brands

Pedal curates vehicles from five trusted brands, each with multiple models to fit different needs and budgets.

| Brand | Models available | Price range (per day) |
|---|---|---|
| Honda | Civic, Accord, CR-V | S/. 800 – S/. 950 |
| Chevrolet | Spark, Cruze, Trailblazer | S/. 700 – S/. 1,000 |
| Volvo | XC40, S60, XC90 | S/. 1,100 – S/. 1,500 |
| Mitsubishi | Mirage, Lancer, Pajero | S/. 600 – S/. 1,400 |
| Volkswagen | Polo, Passat, Tiguan | S/. 700 – S/. 1,200 |

## Service regions

Pedal currently operates in five departments across Peru:

- **Lima** — Multiple pickup points in Miraflores, San Isidro, and San Miguel
- **Ica**
- **Arequipa**
- **Ancash**
- **Piura**

<Note>
  Service availability and vehicle inventory may vary by region. Lima offers the most extensive selection with the greatest number of pickup locations.
</Note>
