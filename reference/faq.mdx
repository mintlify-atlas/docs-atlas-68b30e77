## Getting started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Pedal?">
    Pedal is a consumer-facing car rental platform based in Peru. It allows you to browse available vehicles, compare specifications and pricing, and submit a rental booking request online. Pedal offers vehicles from brands including Honda, Chevrolet, Volvo, Mitsubishi, and Volkswagen, with pickup locations across multiple regions of Peru.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where does Pedal operate?">
    Pedal provides car rental services across five regions of Peru:

    - **Lima**
    - **Ica**
    - **Arequipa**
    - **Ancash**
    - **Piura**

    Within Lima, vehicles can be picked up and returned at three locations: **Miraflores**, **San Isidro**, and **San Miguel**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I make a reservation?">
    To make a reservation, use the booking form on the Pedal website. You will need to provide your pickup location, drop-off location, travel dates, and your preferred car type (Compacto, Sedán, SUV, or Camioneta). Once submitted, Pedal will process your request and confirm availability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Vehicles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What car brands are available?">
    Pedal's fleet currently includes vehicles from five brands:

    - **Honda** — Civic, Accord, CR-V
    - **Chevrolet** — Spark, Cruze, Trailblazer
    - **Volvo** — XC40, S60, XC90
    - **Mitsubishi** — Mirage, Lancer, Pajero
    - **Volkswagen** — Polo, Passat, Tiguan

    Daily rates range from S/. 600 (Mitsubishi Mirage) to S/. 1,500 (Volvo XC90).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I choose between automatic and manual transmission?">
    Yes. Pedal offers both automatic and manual transmission vehicles.

    **Manual transmission** models include:
    - Honda Accord
    - Chevrolet Spark
    - Mitsubishi Mirage
    - Volkswagen Polo
    - Volvo S60

    All other models in the fleet are **automatic**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What fuel types are available?">
    Most vehicles in the Pedal fleet run on **petrol**. The following models run on **diesel**:

    - Honda CR-V
    - Chevrolet Trailblazer
    - Volvo XC90
    - Mitsubishi Pajero
    - Volkswagen Passat

    All diesel models in the fleet have automatic transmission.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many passengers can the vehicles accommodate?">
    Vehicle capacity in the Pedal fleet varies by model:

    - **4 seats** — Chevrolet Spark
    - **5 seats** — Honda Civic, Honda Accord, Honda CR-V, Chevrolet Cruze, Volvo XC40, Volvo S60, Mitsubishi Mirage, Mitsubishi Lancer, Volkswagen Polo, Volkswagen Passat
    - **7 seats** — Chevrolet Trailblazer, Volvo XC90, Mitsubishi Pajero, Volkswagen Tiguan

    If you are traveling with a large group, the Chevrolet Trailblazer, Volvo XC90, Mitsubishi Pajero, and Volkswagen Tiguan are the best options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Booking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What locations can I pick up and drop off my car?">
    Pedal operates across five regions: Lima, Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura.

    Within **Lima**, the available pickup and drop-off points are:
    - Miraflores
    - San Isidro
    - San Miguel

    Specific pickup points in Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura are confirmed at the time of booking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pick up in one location and return to a different one?">
    The booking form allows you to specify separate pickup and drop-off locations. Whether one-way rentals between regions are available depends on the specific locations selected. For confirmation, contact Pedal customer service at serviciopedal@pedal.pe or call (511) 204 4400.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information do I need to complete the booking form?">
    To complete a booking on Pedal you will need to provide:

    - Pickup location
    - Drop-off location
    - Pickup date
    - Return date
    - Preferred car type (Compacto, Sedán, SUV, or Camioneta)

    Additional identification or driver's license details may be required to finalize your reservation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What car types can I choose from?">
    The Pedal booking form offers four rental categories:

    - **Compacto (Compact)** — small, fuel-efficient vehicles for city driving
    - **Sedán** — classic four-door cars for comfortable city or intercity travel
    - **SUV** — larger vehicles with more space and higher ground clearance
    - **Camioneta (Pickup)** — pickup trucks for cargo transport or rural routes

    See the [vehicle types reference](/reference/vehicle-types) for full descriptions of each category.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
