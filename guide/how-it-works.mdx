Renting a car through Pedal is designed to be straightforward. The **Cómo Funciona** section on the homepage walks through the process in three steps, each with an icon, a title, and a short description.

## Before you start

Before going through the rental flow, have the following ready:

- **A pickup location** — Pedal has locations across Peru. Know the general area where you want to collect the vehicle.
- **Your dates and times** — Have your intended rental start and end dates in mind, including the time of day for pickup.
- **Your vehicle preference** — Browse the [vehicle catalog](#topCars) in advance so you know which car or category suits your needs.

<Tip>
  You can filter the vehicle catalog by brand on the Alquiler section of the homepage before committing to a booking.
</Tip>

## Rental steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Seleccionar un lugar">
    Choose your pickup location from Pedal's network of locations across Peru. Multiple pickup points are available in different departments, so you can select the one that is most convenient for your trip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Establecer una fecha">
    Set your rental dates and times. You can specify both the pickup date and time and the return date and time, giving you flexibility to plan around your schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recoger su auto">
    Go to your selected location at the scheduled time. The Pedal team will be on site to assist you with the handover and answer any questions before you set off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Flexibility

<Note>
  Pedal's booking flow lets you adjust dates and locations before finalizing. If your plans change, review your reservation details early to avoid any last-minute complications.
</Note>

Because pickup locations are spread across multiple departments in Peru, most travelers can find a location close to their starting point or destination without needing to travel far to collect a vehicle.
