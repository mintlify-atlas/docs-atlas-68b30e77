## Newsletter

Subscribe to the Pedal newsletter to receive exclusive promotions, new vehicle announcements, and service updates directly in your inbox.

### How to subscribe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the contact section">
    Scroll to the **Contáctanos** (`#contact`) section at the bottom of the Pedal homepage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type your email address into the subscription input field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the terms">
    Check the acceptance checkbox to confirm you agree to Pedal's privacy policy and service conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit the form. You will begin receiving Pedal's newsletter at the address you provided.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must check the terms acceptance checkbox before submitting. The form will not process your subscription without it.
</Warning>

### What you receive

Once subscribed, you will receive emails covering:

- Exclusive rental promotions and seasonal discounts
- New vehicles added to the Pedal fleet
- Service announcements and operational updates

### Terms acceptance

By subscribing, you confirm that you have read and agree to Pedal's privacy policy and service conditions. These documents are accessible via the footer links listed below.
