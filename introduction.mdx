## What is Pedal?

Pedal is a consumer-facing car rental platform built for drivers in Peru. The platform lets you browse a curated fleet of vehicles, filter by brand, and submit a reservation directly from the homepage — all in a few clicks.

The site is available in Spanish and serves customers across five Peruvian departments: Lima, Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura. Pickup and drop-off locations are concentrated in Lima's key districts: Miraflores, San Isidro, and San Miguel.

<Note>
  Pedal's booking form, vehicle catalog, and service map are all accessible from the homepage without creating an account. Simply visit the site, choose your vehicle, and fill in your reservation details.
</Note>

## Vehicle catalog

The catalog section (labeled **Alquiler** in the nav) displays vehicles grouped by brand. By default, Honda vehicles are shown first. Click any brand button to filter the catalog to that brand's models.

Available brands and their models:

| Brand | Models | Price range (per day) |
|---|---|---|
| Honda | Civic, Accord, CR-V | S/. 800 – S/. 950 |
| Chevrolet | Spark, Cruze, Trailblazer | S/. 700 – S/. 1,000 |
| Volvo | XC40, S60, XC90 | S/. 1,100 – S/. 1,500 |
| Mitsubishi | Mirage, Lancer, Pajero | S/. 600 – S/. 1,400 |
| Volkswagen | Polo, Passat, Tiguan | S/. 700 – S/. 1,200 |

Each vehicle card shows the fuel type (Petrol or Diesel), seating capacity, transmission type (Automático or Manual), and fuel efficiency in km per liter.

## Booking form

The booking form sits on the homepage and collects all the information needed for a reservation. It has six fields:

- **Car type** — Compacto, Sedán, SUV, or Camioneta
- **Usage date** — The calendar date for your rental
- **Pickup time** — The time you'll collect the vehicle
- **Pickup location** — Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel
- **Return time** — The time you'll bring the vehicle back
- **Return location** — Miraflores, San Isidro, or San Miguel

## How the rental process works

Pedal describes its rental flow in three steps:

1. **Select a location** — Choose where you want to pick up your vehicle from Pedal's locations across Peru.
2. **Set a date** — Specify the dates and times you need the car. Pedal offers flexibility to match your travel schedule.
3. **Pick up your car** — Arrive at the chosen location at the scheduled time. Pedal's team will be ready to hand over the vehicle.

## Service coverage

Pedal currently operates in five departments across Peru:

- **Lima** — The primary service region, with pickup locations in Miraflores, San Isidro, and San Miguel
- **Ica**
- **Arequipa**
- **Ancash**
- **Piura**

<Note>
  Vehicle availability and pickup options vary by region. Lima has the most extensive selection and the only confirmed per-district pickup points.
</Note>
