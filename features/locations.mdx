## Lima pickup points

All three Lima locations are in well-connected, central districts that are easily accessible by taxi, public transit, and private vehicle.

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Miraflores" icon="map-pin">
    A coastal commercial district in the south of Lima. Well-served by major avenues and close to the Malecón and financial district.
  </Card>
  <Card title="San Isidro" icon="map-pin">
    Lima's main business district, centrally located with access to the Javier Prado and Arequipa corridors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="San Miguel" icon="map-pin">
    Located on the western edge of Lima, near the airport road and the Costa Verde expressway.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Tip>
  If you are arriving at Jorge Chávez International Airport, San Miguel is the closest of the three pickup locations and is accessible via the Costanera highway.
</Tip>

## Interactive map

The Pedal locations page embeds a Google Maps view centred on Lima, allowing you to visually confirm the general service area before booking. The map is displayed directly on the website and does not require a separate application.

Use the map to:
- Orient yourself relative to your hotel or destination.
- Estimate travel time to the nearest pickup district.
- Confirm that your itinerary stays within the Lima coverage zone.

## Department coverage modal

On the locations page, a **"Ver lista aquí"** (View list here) link opens a modal overlay listing all departments where Pedal operates. This provides a quick reference without leaving the page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the coverage section">
    Scroll to the service regions section on the locations page. You will see a brief description of Pedal's national coverage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the department list">
    Click **"Ver lista aquí"** to open the modal. The overlay lists all five active departments: Lima, Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the modal">
    Click outside the modal or use the close button to dismiss it and return to the locations page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choosing a convenient location

<Tip>
  If you plan to drive south along the Pan-American Highway toward Ica or Arequipa, starting from Miraflores saves you driving through the city centre.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  For trips heading north to Ancash or Piura, picking up in San Isidro or San Miguel puts you closer to the northern arterial roads.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  You can return your vehicle to a different location from where you picked it up. Use this flexibility if your schedule ends in a different district than where you started.
</Tip>
