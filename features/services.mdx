## Strategic location network

Pedal's three Lima locations are chosen to cover the most-travelled corridors in the metropolitan area:

| District | Characteristics |
|---|---|
| Miraflores | Coastal district with major commercial avenues and coastal road access |
| San Isidro | Central business district; access to Javier Prado and Arequipa corridors |
| San Miguel | Western Lima; close to the airport road and Costa Verde expressway |

Beyond Lima, Pedal covers four additional departments — Ica, Arequipa, Ancash, and Piura. Contact Pedal directly to arrange rentals outside Lima.

## Efficient, fast service commitment

Pedal's booking flow is designed to take you from selection to confirmation with minimal friction:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse and filter">
    Use the brand filter in the vehicle catalog to identify the right model for your trip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the booking form">
    Fill in six fields — car type, date, pickup time, pickup location, return time, and return location — and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive confirmation">
    Pedal's team confirms availability and contacts you to finalise the reservation details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect your vehicle">
    Arrive at your chosen pickup district at the agreed time and collect your vehicle with minimal paperwork.
  </Step>
</Steps>
