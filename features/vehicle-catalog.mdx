Pedal offers a curated fleet of 15 vehicles across five brands. Use the brand filter buttons on the homepage to narrow the catalog to the make you prefer, then review each model's specs and daily rate before proceeding to the booking form.

## Brand filter system

The catalog page displays five filter buttons, one per partner brand:

<Columns cols={5}>
  <Card title="Honda" icon="car" />
  <Card title="Chevrolet" icon="car" />
  <Card title="Volvo" icon="car" />
  <Card title="Mitsubishi" icon="car" />
  <Card title="Volkswagen" icon="car" />
</Columns>

Clicking a brand button hides all vehicles that do not belong to that brand. Clicking the same button again (or the "All" option if present) restores the full listing. Only one filter is active at a time.

## How to use the brand filter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the vehicle catalog">
    Navigate to the main page and scroll to the vehicle catalog section. All 15 vehicles are displayed by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a brand">
    Click one of the five brand buttons — Honda, Chevrolet, Volvo, Mitsubishi, or Volkswagen — to filter the listing to that brand only. The inactive vehicles are hidden immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compare models">
    Review the visible cards side by side. Each card shows the model name, seating capacity, transmission type, fuel efficiency, and daily rate in Soles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset the filter">
    Click the active brand button again to clear the filter and return to the full catalog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for choosing the right vehicle

<Tip>
  If you are travelling with six or more passengers, filter by brands that offer 7-seat models: Chevrolet Trailblazer, Volvo XC90, Mitsubishi Pajero, or Volkswagen Tiguan.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Diesel models (Honda CR-V, Chevrolet Trailblazer, Volvo XC90, Mitsubishi Pajero, Volkswagen Passat) tend to have lower fuel costs per kilometre on long-distance routes to regions such as Arequipa or Piura.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  For city driving in Lima where traffic is frequent, favour the most fuel-efficient models — the Volkswagen Polo (18 km/lt) and Mitsubishi Mirage (17 km/lt) offer the best economy at the lowest daily rates.
</Tip>

<Note>
  All prices are in Peruvian Soles (S/.) and represent the daily rental rate. Final cost depends on the number of rental days selected in the booking form.
</Note>
